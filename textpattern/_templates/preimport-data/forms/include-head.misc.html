Welcome to Mosman Municipal Council

Located on the northern shores of Sydney Harbour, Mosman offers a rich cultural life amidst beautiful bushland surrounds. Mosman Council plans for building and development, maintains parks, beaches and sporting facilities, provides services for children, youth and seniors and runs cultural activities, a library and an art gallery.
